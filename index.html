About Fiberglass Technical Group

Leading the industry in reactive metal fabrication expertise and chemical processing solutions

Decades of Expertise in Reactive Metals

Fiberglass Technical Group has been at the forefront of reactive metal fabrication and inspection services for the chemical processing industry. Our deep expertise in titanium, zirconium, and tantalum systems has made us the trusted partner for major corporations including Albemarle Corporation.

We specialize in the most challenging environments where standard materials fail. Our team of certified engineers and inspectors ensures that every project meets the highest standards of quality, safety, and performance.

From initial design consultation to final inspection and ongoing maintenance support, we provide comprehensive solutions that keep your operations running safely and efficiently.
